5G NR Channel Estimation using Deep Learning

Trained a Convolutional Neural Network to estimate the wireless channel in a 5G NR OFDM link — targeting fast-moving receivers in weak-signal conditions where classical LS/MMSE estimators struggle.

Generated 50,000+ labelled training samples by simulating multipath Rayleigh fading channels in MATLAB at varying mobility and SNR levels.
Achieved ~15% lower Bit Error Rate compared to standard MMSE estimation at 10 dB SNR.
Designed CNN architecture in TensorFlow, trained on GPU, evaluated against MATLAB 5G Toolbox baselines.

⚙️ FPGA Aug 2025 – Nov 2025

FPGA-Based Digital FIR Filter Design

Designed a 32-tap FIR low-pass filter in Verilog HDL for real-time audio denoising on a Xilinx Artix-7 FPGA, cross-checking output against MATLAB-generated filter coefficients.

Reworked the Multiply-Accumulate (MAC) unit with a pipelined architecture, cutting resource usage by 18% and pushing max clock speed to 120 MHz.
Wrote comprehensive testbenches in Vivado to confirm frequency response matched the theoretical design exactly.
Validated stopband attenuation of -80 dB with 0.5 dB passband ripple on hardware.
